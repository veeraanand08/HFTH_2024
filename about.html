About Us

Team PixelCode is a hackathon team hailing from Langley High Shcool. Its members consist of Zackery Gast, Veera Anand, and Oscar Anderson. Zack works in Java, HTML, and CSS, and parts of the pixel art. Veera works in Java, C++, Python, Javascrip, Django, and also does pixel art. Oscar works in HTML, CSS, Java, C++, C#, Xcode, and does pixel art.
