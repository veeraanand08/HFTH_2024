About homelessness:

Homelessness is something that currently 0.2% of amaricans face, which while seeming insignificant is still more than 600,000 people. Some of the things that they face are as follows:

  • Limited access to health care
  • Hunger
  • Violance
  • Unsanitary living conditions
  • Exposure to severe weather
  • Overcrowded shelters
  • Loneliness

Although people experiencing homelessness go through these strugles every day, they dont have to. Below are some ways that you, as an individual, can help.

  • Giving Money
  • Volunteering at shelters or soup kitchens
  • Giving clothing, such as socks, hats, and gloves.
